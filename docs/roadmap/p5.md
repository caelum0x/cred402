# p5 — Omnichain credit (satellites live)

> **Agents earn anywhere, borrow anywhere — Casper decides who is creditworthy.**
> Activate the cross-chain primitives so an agent's x402 revenue on any chain
> anchors to Casper, and satellite credit vaults lend only against Casper-issued
> authorization within a global exposure cap.

## Why
Agents already operate across chains. Fragmenting reputation per chain kills the
product. Casper stays the canonical root of identity, receipts, reputation, and
credit policy; other chains become execution + liquidity satellites that report
back. The standards (CAID/ABE/URE/CAN) and contracts already exist — this phase
turns them on in production.

## Scope
- **Address binding.** `AddressBindingRegistry` live: agents prove control of
  EVM/Solana/Cosmos addresses via dual signatures (no external credit yet).
- **External receipt anchoring.** EVM `ReceiptOutbox` → relayer → Casper
  `ExternalReceiptRegistry`; revenue earned on Base/etc. settles reputation on Casper.
- **Casper-issued credit.** `GlobalExposureManager` + `CreditAuthorizationNotes`
  live: Casper reserves global exposure and issues short-lived, policy-signed CANs;
  satellite `CreditVault`s lend **only** against a valid CAN — the cross-chain
  over-borrow guard.
- **Trust ladder.** Launch at Stage 1 (trusted relayer + public logs) → Stage 2
  (bonded multi-relayer + challenge window). Reconciliation service for
  draws/repayments/defaults.

## Deliverables
- Base Sepolia → mainnet EVM satellite (CreditVault, ExposureReporter, mirrors)
  deployed + wired to the live relayer + proof service.
- AddressBinding + ExternalReceipt + CAN flow live end-to-end (extend `demo:multichain`
  to real chains).
- Reconciliation + exposure dashboards.

## Success metric
An agent **borrows on Base** against a Casper-issued CAN, within its Casper-rooted
global cap, and the repayment reconciles back to Casper — with no double-borrow possible.

## Out of scope
zk/light-client proofs (p7), full DAO governance (p6).
